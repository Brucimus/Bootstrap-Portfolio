About Me
Junior Web Developer that specializes in MERN Stack. I believe that communication is the backbone of a strong organization, and I developed an automated processes and reporting method in my last position that helped reduce miscommunications as well as task time consumption. What started as a small Access and Excel project to make my corporate role more efficient, turned into a cross account and branch data management system.

That project inspired me to earn a Full-stack certification from UCLA’s Extension Bootcamp where I advanced my knowledge of Javascript, SQL, and Node.js, amongst others, to create even more dynamic and efficient methods to help people communicate. I strongly believe in automated reporting and data-driven decision making, and that just because something works doesn’t mean it cannot be made better. It is that kind of thinking combined with my skill set that makes me a quality web developer and team member.
